Welcome to JoeCrop.com



Hi,

My name is Joe Crop, I am currently a digital IC design engineer at Maxim Integrated (now Analog Devices). At Maxim I work on a mixed-signal design team where I design IP for SoCs such as ADCs and PLLs and help drive design/verification methodology.

I completed my PhD in electrical engineering at Oregon State University in 2014. As a member of the VLSI research group I worked on ultra-low power, resilient, variation tolerant digital integrated circuits.

My research interests include exploring methods to reduce power in digital circuits as well as improving the reliability and robustness of ultra-low power digital circuits. On top of these techniques I explore the use of analog circuits to improve the operation of sub/near-threshold digital circuits.

I have always had a passion for web design. Recently I have started a company that services the ed-tech market: GatherAct LLC.

Please feel free to explore this site and check out what I'm up to.
